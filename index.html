About Me

I'm a self taught web designer and developer based in Boston, MA. I began to build an interest in computers at a young age of ten, attending a computer camp where I built my first website using HTML. It was basic, and although I tinkered with HTML all through my childhood, I never seriously pursued it until more recently.

After graduating college, I worked in fundraising for nonprofits. However, I became frustrated in how slow many nonprofits were to adopt new technologies. I then started to delve into different tech skills and learn more about email marketing. I began my journey into back into tech by re-teaching myself HTML and was instantly hooked. I quickly took up learning JavaScript and never looked back. Since then, I have worked on many small projects that you can see below. I am excited to pursue a career in web design and ultimately hope to integrate new technologies into nonprofits or other companies to improve life for people.

Languages and Frameworks:
Javascript, HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Bootstrap3.
Tools & expertise:
Git, Responsive Web Design.
